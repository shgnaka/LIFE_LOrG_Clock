# Scoring Reference (Impact x Effort)

## Impact

- `High`: broad user or reliability impact; blocks delivery.
- `Medium`: visible quality or maintainability gain.
- `Low`: localized cleanup.

## Effort

- `S`: small change, low blast radius.
- `M`: multi-file change, moderate validation.
- `L`: architecture/migration work.

## Priority Mapping

- `P1`: High impact with S/M effort.
- `P2`: Medium impact or high effort tradeoff.
- `P3`: Low impact and/or opportunistic cleanup.
